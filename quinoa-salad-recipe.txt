1 cup quinoa (rinsed) 
2 cups broth (weak broth, if you are using pre made, dilute it) 
spring onions (1/4-1/2 cup) 
feta (1/2 cup, crumbled) 
raisins (1/4-1/2 cup) 
lemon juice (4tbsp) 
olive oil (3tbsp) 
garlic (3 cloves) 
Basil paste (2 tbsp) 
Mix garlic, lemon, oil together, add raisins and let sit Cook quionoa in broth (don't over cook quinoa) Mix quiona, feta and spring onions Mix basil paste in with raisins Mix raisins in with salad
